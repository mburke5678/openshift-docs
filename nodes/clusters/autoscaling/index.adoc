= Overview of autoscaling your cluster
include::_attributes/common-attributes.adoc[]
:context: overview-of-nodes

toc::[]


There are different three options you can use to scale your cluster. You can either scale either your workload, the number of pods or the resources attached to those pods, or the number of nodes in your cluster.

You can autoscale your pods based on CPU or memory usage.
